Superclock is an ongoing web experiment aimed to control your devices over wifi from any browser. The prototype is 3D printed with some electronics inside: Arduino Yun, RGB led, motor and more coming soon.

We provide a code free toolkit to control your devices. This work is currently in progress, but feel free to fork it, to contribute or to leave comments.
